About Us

A little about Us

Welcome to Aapni Dharohar

Rajasthan, the land of rich culture and heritage, is home to various artisans communities who create beautiful and int ricate handicraft items. However, these communities are currently facing a crisis as their livelihoods are at the brink of extinction. To combat this issue, we are proud to announce a hackathon that aims to create a website that brings to gether all of these artist communities on one platform. This website will feature the products of these artisans, giving them a wider audience and the opportunity to generate more revenue. We hope that by promoting their art forms, we can encourage more people to appreciate and purchas e these precious and delicate products.

"Heritage of several communities for over 300 years".”

BLogs on artworks:

As an art lover, you will have the opportunity to visit our website and order these products directly from the artisans. Not only will you get to own a piece of Rajasthan's rich cultural heritage, but you will also be supporting the livelih oods of these communities. We hope that our website will be able to make a positive impact on these artisans' lives a nd help preserve their traditional art forms for future generations. Additionally, the website will be designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, allowing customers to search and filter products based on their preferences. The website will also provide detailed information about the artisans, thei r communities, and the traditional techniques used in their craft. This way, customers will be able to understand the c ultural significance of their purchases and appreciate the artisans’ work even more.
