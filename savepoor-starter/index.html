    Our Mission and Goals

    Encourage preservation of traditional techniques.

    support traditional artisans in preserving their unique techniques and practices. This would involve providing funding for apprenticeships, workshops, and other educational programs that help pass down knowledge to future generations.

    Develop new markets for artisanal products.

    Another goal could be to help traditional artisan communities access new markets for their products. This could involve connecting artisans with retailers, creating e-commerce platforms, or organizing craft fairs and markets.

    Increase the visibility of traditional artisans

    Traditional artisans often lack the visibility and recognition they deserve. A goal could be to raise awareness of these communities through social media campaigns, documentaries, and other forms of media.

    Support sustainable practices.

    Traditional artisan communities often have a deep respect for the environment and use sustainable materials and practices. Supporting these practices can help promote environmentally responsible production.

    Foster community engagement.

    Encouraging community engagement could help build support and increase awareness of traditional artisan communities. This could involve hosting events, workshops, or other activities that bring together artisans, community members, and other stakeholders..

    Encourage policy support

    Government policies can have a significant impact on the livelihoods of traditional artisans. Advocating for policies that support these communities, such as tax incentives, subsidies, and legal protections, could be an important goal.
